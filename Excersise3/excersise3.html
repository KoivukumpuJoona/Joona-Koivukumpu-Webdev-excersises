Tyrendarra

Tyrendarra /ˈtɪərəndərə/ is a locality in south west Victoria, Australia. The locality is split between the Shire of Glenelg and the Shire of Moyne local government areas, with most being in the former.

It is on the Princes Highway, 338 kilometres (210 mi) south west of the state capital, Melbourne.
